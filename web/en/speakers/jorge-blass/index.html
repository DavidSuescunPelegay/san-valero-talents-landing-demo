HOME > SPEAKERS > Jorge Blass

Illusionist

Jorge
Blass

Jorge Blass (Madrid 1980). Illusionist and Spanish communicator. As an illusionist he has obtained some of the most prestigious international awards: at the age of 19, he received the Magical Gold Wand, given by S.M. Prince Rainier of Monaco and the Sarmoti Prize which was given by the two actors Siegfried and Roy in Las Vegas, USA. On television, he has presented and directed three seasons of the Magic program “Nada x aquí” (four) and has collaborated on the TV show “Maters of Illusion” on the American TV FOX. In 2009 Jorge presented his work at the prestigious Kodak Theater in Los Angeles with the American TV star Neil Patrick Harris. His next television project is “Por arte de Magia”, in which he collaborates and is the co-director who has made an adaptation for the spanish TV channel  Antena3.

CREATE UNFORGETTABLE EXPERIENCES IN ENTERTAINMENT

  • Keynote
  • Wed 19 | 19:00h
  • Spanish
  • ES/EN

Jorge Blass, renowned international illusionist, will share his creative process to generate a WOW effect among the audience.  

Only those who are able to generate memorable experiences will be able to have an effect on the audience, we will dive deep into the entertainment world together with Jorge to learn about the methods and procedures that allow him to create magical emotions.

David Copperfield commented the following: “Jorge Blass elevates the art of magic to a new level”, we will discover why at Experience Fighters…

SOBRE NOSOTROS

Experience Fighters 2019 es la sexta edición del principal evento anual sobre creación de experiencias e innovación de España, donde se combinan el diseño de servicios, el diseño de experiencias, la experiencia y la tecnología. Este año volveremos a Madrid con un enfoque renovado: sede con más capacidad, nuevos servicios, oradores internacionales que siempre hemos querido ver en nuestro país y una comunidad que se conecta antes, durante y después del evento.
